About Us

You request it? We have it.

Top Tail Groomers specialises in professional & premium Mobile House-call pets grooming services island wide in Singapore.

The concept of mobile home dog & cat grooming has never been more prominent in Singapore than the West. In line with the increased demands of home pets grooming, Top Tail Groomers was established with the belief that our client's pets' needs are of the utmost importance.

We provide quality dog & cat grooming services, conducted right in your home as a premium and personalized service for your pets in its own familiar and comfortable environment, away from hazards and stress-inducing factors.
